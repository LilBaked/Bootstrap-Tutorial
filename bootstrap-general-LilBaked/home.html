Our Mission

Inkless is a start-up social media website where poets can express themselves freely. We plan to offer our members many ways to interact with each other, create and evolve their own writing style, and overall just a fun and cool experience. Since the site is only in it's early stages, we are working day and night to make our mission come true. Our biggest goal would be creating and managing a pure and cool community where we can all explore the art of poetry.

Our Background

It first started simple, a few verses written on pages or via phone, then we learned to implement our opinions, emotions and philosophical thoughts. After many poems here we are, not that we consider ourselves professionals, but we knew there is something unique about our art, We are two university students from Romania, we like expressing ourselves in many other ways, including music and drawing We wanted to share what we do with the world, and not only share it, but also invite other people to share their art with us. Ultimately, we are doing this out of fun and pure curiosity, it's not only a project, but an experiment.
